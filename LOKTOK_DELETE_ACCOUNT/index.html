Account Deletion Request

LokTok - Smart Door Lock Management App
Last updated: 19-01-2026

DBS SECURITY SYSTEMS PVT. LTD. operates the LokTok mobile application. This page explains how users can request deletion of their LokTok account and associated personal data.

How to Delete Your LokTok Account

You can delete your LokTok account using one of the following methods:

Option 1: Delete Account Within the App

  • Open the LokTok app
  • Log in using your registered email
  • Open Home Screen Drawer
  • Go to Settings
  • Select Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion

Option 2: Request Account Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us via email:

  • Email: naman@dbsindia.in
  • Subject: LokTok Account Deletion Request
  • Please include your registered email address

What Data Is Deleted

Once your account deletion request is processed, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account credentials
  • Smart lock access permissions
  • User-related configuration data

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Your personal data is permanently removed from our systems
Important:
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, you will no longer be able to access your account or smart lock data.
